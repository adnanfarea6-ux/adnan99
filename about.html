About TimeZone

Crafting exceptional timepieces since 1985

Our Story

TimeZone was founded with a simple vision: to create timepieces that combine traditional craftsmanship with modern innovation. For over three decades, we have been dedicated to producing watches that not only tell time but tell a story of excellence, precision, and timeless style.

Our journey began in a small workshop where master craftsmen meticulously assembled each watch by hand. Today, while we have grown significantly, we maintain the same commitment to quality and attention to detail that has defined our brand from the beginning.
