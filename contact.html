Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn watches in original packaging. Please contact our customer service team to initiate a return.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Please check our shipping page for detailed information.

What warranty do you provide?

All TimeZone watches come with a 2-year international warranty covering manufacturing defects. Water damage and normal wear are not covered.

Can I get my watch serviced?

Yes, we provide professional watch servicing. Contact us to schedule an appointment or mail your watch to our service center.
